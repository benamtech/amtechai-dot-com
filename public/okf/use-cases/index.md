# Use cases

The operational AI use cases (UC1–UC10) that articles and playbooks connect to.

* [Business Brain](/use-cases/uc1-business-brain.md) - The operational knowledge backbone everything else runs on.
* [Full AI operations employee](/use-cases/uc10-full-operations-employee.md) - A connected back office spanning several operational use cases at once.
* [Purchasing intelligence](/use-cases/uc2-purchasing-intelligence.md) - Reads sales plus open repairs and orders and creates supplier reorder lists.
* [Demand forecasting](/use-cases/uc3-demand-forecasting.md) - Uses sales history and seasonality to plan stock and buying ahead.
* [Transaction and margin analysis](/use-cases/uc4-margin-analysis.md) - Reviews every sale and job for margin leaks, pricing gaps, and outliers.
* [Job and repair pipeline planning](/use-cases/uc5-pipeline-planning.md) - Turns next week's work into required parts, labor hours, and prep.
* [Quoting and takeoff](/use-cases/uc6-quoting-and-takeoff.md) - Turns specs, designs, plans, and live parts pricing into quotes and material lists.
* [Supplier and vendor management](/use-cases/uc7-vendor-management.md) - Tracks pricing, lead times, reorder timing, and vendor performance.
* [Financial ops and owner briefing](/use-cases/uc8-owner-briefing.md) - Categorizes, reconciles, and produces weekly owner action reports.
* [Service-history intelligence](/use-cases/uc9-service-history.md) - Knows each client's equipment, service history, and what is due.
