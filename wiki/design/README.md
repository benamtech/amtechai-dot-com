# AMTECH Design Wiki

## Purpose

This directory is the working design constitution for AMTECH's education-first website. It translates the current conversion pages, visual critique, and strategic content direction into reusable rules for pages, articles, symbols, and future component work.

## North Star

AMTECH teaches ambitious small and medium business operators how to install AI into the practical work of the business.

The site should feel like a 1970s industrial field manual crossed with a modern AI operating system: calm authority, no hype, no softness, no generic AI decoration. The future is here; serious operators learn the system.

- `/sales-bootcamp` is the best current template for high-conviction education-to-conversion sequencing.
- `/wholesale-2` is the best current operator-program concept and should become the model for field-guide funnels.
- `/sell-ai-employees` is the seed of a distinct internal briefing format for sales enablement.

## Article strategy

AMTECH should not publish blog posts. It should publish operator procedures: specific, indexed, useful business instructions that show where AI fits into an existing workflow.

Every education page should answer:

1. What business task is being improved?
2. Who is the operator?
3. What does the human do?
4. What does the AI do?
5. What inputs are required?
6. What output does the business receive?
7. What can go wrong?
8. What should the operator implement next?

## Rules for future article design

- Treat every page as a field document, not a marketing blog layout.
- Use stark black, white, AMTECH red, and utility grays only unless a new color has a documented semantic role.
- Prefer mono labels, hard rules, tables, numbered steps, and diagram panels over soft cards.
- Use red as a signal: system emphasis, section punctuation, active state, CTA, warning, or proof accent.
- Use symbols to explain action, not to decorate.
- Use specific industry/task metadata so articles behave like an indexed operating library.
- Avoid generic AI imagery, abstract stock images, and soft SaaS illustration tropes.
- The operator is the hero; AI is the instrument.
