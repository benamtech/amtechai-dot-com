# Article Field Guide Template

## Purpose

This template is for scalable AMTECH education pages. The goal is to publish hundreds of pages without producing generic SEO content. Each page should be a specific procedure a business operator can understand and act on.

## Metadata

- Industry:
- Task:
- Business size:
- Operator:
- AI skill/agent:
- Estimated time saved:
- Human approval required:
- Primary entity:
- Related entities:
- Service area / geography, if local:
- Funnel action:

## Hero

Use a specific promise, not an abstract thought-leadership headline.

Example: `How a painting company can create and send estimates with an AI agent.`

Required elements:

- Mono document label, such as `FIELD GUIDE / PAINTING / ESTIMATES`.
- Manifesto headline.
- One-paragraph business context.
- Metadata strip: industry, task, operator, output, human approval.
- Primary CTA framed as the next assignment.

## The Old Way

Describe the current manual workflow. Name the friction: duplicate entry, missed calls, late estimates, owner bottlenecks, payroll confusion, untracked follow-up.

## The New System

Describe the AI-assisted workflow in plain operational language. Do not say AI magically solves it. Explain what the system reads, generates, routes, reminds, or updates.

## Inputs Required

List the data, tools, permissions, and templates needed before the procedure can run.

Recommended format:

| Input | Source | Owner | Required? |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Customer request | Form, call, SMS | Office manager | Yes |
| Pricing rules | Estimate template | Owner | Yes |
| Job photos | Field tech | Operator | Optional |

## Procedure

Use numbered steps. Each step should state:

- Human action
- Machine action
- Output
- Review requirement

## Human Review Points

Make approval explicit. AMTECH content should build trust by showing where the human remains in control.

## Risks

Include compliance, customer experience, quality control, data quality, and exception-handling notes.

## Output

Name what the business receives: estimate, invoice, payroll summary, booked appointment, CRM update, callback list, review request queue.

## Implementation Checklist

Use an operator-ready checklist with concrete actions.

## Related Guides

Internal links should strengthen topical/entity structure:

- Related industry hub
- Related AI skill page
- Related procedure
- Related system diagram
- Related operator checklist

## Visual modules

- Procedure diagram
- Before/after workflow strip
- Human does / AI does split panel
- Industry-specific checklist
- Risk and exception table
- Glossary/entity block for SEO
- Output specimen panel

## SEO / knowledge graph guidance

Every article should expose structured relationships:

- Industry -> task -> workflow -> AI skill -> output -> business role.
- Use consistent names for entities across pages.
- Link articles into hubs by industry, task, and AI skill.
- Prefer exact operational titles over vague SEO phrasing.
- Use glossary blocks to define business objects and AI skills in consistent language.
