# Wholesale 2 Design Review

## Role in the system

`/wholesale-2` has the strongest operator-program concept: a concrete market, a painful workflow, an AI-assisted operating system, a dashboard, and a beta/application frame.

## Strong elements

- The promise is direct: break into wholesaling without manual cold calls.
- The 73% statistic can become an evidence plate.
- The call-volume interruption section dramatizes the operational pain.
- The membership content explains infrastructure rather than selling vague benefits.
- The dashboard visual points toward an instrument-panel language.
- The beta cohort creates seriousness and scarcity.

## Recommended industrial field-guide refinements

- Frame the hero as a cover page for an operator field guide.
- Frame the 73% section as `EVIDENCE PLATE 01`.
- Frame the dashboard as `SYSTEM DIAGRAM 02` or `INSTRUMENT PANEL 02`.
- Frame how-it-works as a procedure diagram.
- Frame the beta section as an application notice.
- Add numbered marginal labels such as `FIELD NOTE 01`, `SYSTEM DIAGRAM 02`, and `OPERATOR REQUIREMENT 03`.

## Color and hierarchy cleanup

- Replace non-system accent colors such as orange with AMTECH red unless assigned a semantic role.
- Keep green only for completed machine status or success states.
- Align spacing, max-widths, and header rules with the more disciplined rhythm of `/sales-bootcamp`.
- Reduce assembled landing-page feeling by using document conventions: cover, evidence, procedure, instrument panel, application notice.
