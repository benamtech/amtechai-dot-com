# Sell AI Employees Design Review

## Role in the system

`/sell-ai-employees` should become AMTECH's internal briefing format. It is not a public sales page; it is a sales-room document.

## Current strength

The existing page already declares `Rep briefing - internal` and `For the closer. Not for prospects.` That label is the seed of a reusable content type.

## Recommended briefing conventions

- Briefing number
- Audience label
- Distribution note
- Objective
- Call frame
- Offer stack
- Objection map
- Proof language
- Closing checklist
- Approved talk tracks
- Forbidden claims

## Visual distinction from public pages

- Tighter grid density.
- More memo-like hierarchy.
- More small labels and document metadata.
- Fewer public persuasion flourishes.
- Clear internal-use marks.
- Stronger black/white/red hierarchy with scarlet reserved for priority instructions.

## Future content type

Use `Briefings` for sales enablement, partner enablement, internal training, launch memos, and objection-handling documents.
